My Story

Hi there! My name is Roho and I'm a versatile designer with a passion for bringing ideas to life. With years of experience in animation, I love designing interactive experiences that engage and captivate users.

I first discovered my passion for design and animation when I was very young. I have been working with the Adobe Suite ever since. However, during my higher education, I decided to study science to gain a deeper understanding of the natural world. This pursuit of knowledge is what sets me apart - I have a natural talent for learning new skills and integrating them into my work.

Later, my fascination with the mind and behavior led me to study neuroscience, which has uniquely informed my approach to interaction design. Recently, I've been delving into coding and have been teaching myself how to create beautiful and functional frontend interfaces. My strong roots in design and UX have allowed me to seamlessly incorporate coding into my repertoire and create even more dynamic and comprehensive user experiences.
