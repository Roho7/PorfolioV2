PROJECT

Lo-Fi Station

WEB-DEV

  HTML   CSS   Javascript

2023
CONTEXT

I love listening to lo-fi music while working. But searching for the right lo-fi before I started work was very distracting. I could never find the right one and often lost my work mindset when I surfed YouTube for too long. It was also annoying to have my pomodoro on an app and the music playing on my computer. I would be often be completely thrown off with this set-up. The productivity booster was becoming unproductive. So I integrated these two features into this website and made it into an immersive lo-fi experience. It automatically fetches the right video which are pre-sorted according to mood and time of day. It also has a pomodoro feature that gives sound cues so you don't have to check on it between your work.
